Browser extension for learning languages with watching movies and TV shows

Integration with multiple video services

To use the plugin, you must have a subscription to the desired service

Easysubs features

Word and full subtitle translation
Simply hover over the word you want to translate and the video will automatically pause and restart when you move the cursor away from the subtitles.

Click on the subtitles to translate the entire text.
Export words to learning services
You can add new words to any of the supported language learning services.

Subtitles progress bar
There is a subtitle progress bar at the bottom of the player to help you navigate between subtitles.

Click the progress bar to rewind the video to the point you want.
Subtitles settings and custom subtitles
The extension allows you to customise the appearance, position and delay of subtitles.

You can also upload your own subtitles if they are not available on the video service. SRT and VTT formats are supported.
