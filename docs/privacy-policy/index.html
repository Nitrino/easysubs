Privacy Policy

We know absolutely nothing about you and do not store any of your data. We don't track you. Everything you do in our extension belongs only to you and is not sent to our servers.

In all seriousness, privacy and openness are a priority for us. Our code is fully open at Github and is available for review.

What are we building?

No data is being collected at this time

Delete your data

At the moment the application works exclusively in your browser and does not send data to our servers. This way, we just delete nothing.

Browser permissions

Our extension requests two approvals:

1. Access to all visited web pages
This seems to be too broad and unsafe a right, but it is necessary.
Our extension supports several videostreaming services, including kino.pub. This service provides a personal unique domain for each user. Thus, we do not know exactly on what domain our extension will run. Instead, the first thing the application does is try to determine whether this site is one of the supported services and if it is not, it does not execute any code at all. We do not collect information about the pages you visit.

2. Access to Storage
This permission is required to store the plugin settings in your browser. For example, translation language or subtitle display settings.
