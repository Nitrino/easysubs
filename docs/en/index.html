FEATURES

EasySubs is compatible with YouTube, Netflix (subscription required), Coursera, and Kinopub. More video streaming services coming soon!
Translate words and sentences
  • Hover over a word to translate it.
  • Video playback will be automatically paused.
  • Translate the entire phrase by clicking on it.
  • Look up details in Cambridge dictionary.
Save new words

Export words to language learning services:

  • Anki
  • LinguaLeo
  • Puzzle English
Customize subtitles

Do you have .SRT or .VTT subtitle file in your native language?

Upload it and easily sync it with the video if needed.

Multiple features to boost your language learning:

Instant word pronouncation

Prononcation examples from Forvo and Youglish

Lookups in Cambridge and Urban dictionaries

Upload your own subtitles

Manual subtitle sync

Subtitle progress bar

Rewind video by subtitles

Autopause after each sentence

Size and position of subtitles

Works on Windows, Mac and Linux
